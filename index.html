WHO AM I?

My name is Andrea Klvackova but most people know me as Zabka. I am artist and traveller from Slovakia, currently living in Prague, Czech Republic. I had a chance to collaborate with brands such as Vans, Levi’s, Bata, T-Mobile or Horsefeathers. I am open to all collaborations and opportunities to expand my portfolio with interesting projects. Welcome to my colorful website and get inspired!





WHAT DO I DO?

For past four years I’ve been mainly focusing on custom painted sneakers. It all started by coincidence, but then I was approach by Vans and I’ve been painting their iconic shoes ever since. I am also customizing clothes and all kind of extreme sports equipment such as skateboards, skis, snowboards, helmets, etc. If you are interested in owning such a custom made piece, you can fill the form at the order page, if you are curious about my art, please feel free to click on gallery.
